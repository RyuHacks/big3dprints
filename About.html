Our 3D Printing Materials

PETG

  • Stronger, more durable, and more impact-resistant than PLA.
  • Excellent chemical resistance and less brittle nature.
  • Combines flexibility with toughness, ideal for functional parts.
  • *Note:* Generally costs more and may require longer print times than PLA.

PLA

  • Easy to print with a smooth finish; great for beginners.
  • Biodegradable and available in many colors/finishes.
  • Great choice for prototypes, visual models, cosplay props, and other **low-stress applications**.
  • Typically faster to print and more cost-effective than PETG.
