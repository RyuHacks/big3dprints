How It Works

📞

Step 1: Get In Touch

Use our contact form or book an appointment through our calendar. Meetings are encouraged — they help us get your project right.

📁

Step 2: Share Your Idea

Provide your STL file or describe what you want. No file? No problem — we can create simple designs for you at an extra cost.

📏

Step 3: Size Check

We check your model to ensure it fits our printer’s max size: 430 × 430 x 500 mm. We’ll contact you if adjustments are needed.

🖨️

Step 4: Printing & Shipping

We’ll print and ship the model to your address. We don’t offer post-processing — your print arrives as-is.

⏱️

Timeline

From start to delivery, the full process usually takes about 2 - 3 weeks.
